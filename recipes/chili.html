Chili

Description

The family household staple when it get's cold out. Easy and delicious

Ingredients

  • 1lb ground turkey
  • 1 can pinto beans
  • 1 can chili beans
  • 2 cans diced tomatoes
  • 48oz tomato juice
  • 1 packet chili seasoning

Steps

  1. Cook ground turkey in pan
  2. Drain excess fat
  3. Place ground turkey in slow cooker
  4. Drain diced tomatos and beans juices and place in slow cooker
  5. Pour in tomato juice
  6. Stir in chili seasoning packet
  7. Cook on low 6-8 hours, or high 3-4
